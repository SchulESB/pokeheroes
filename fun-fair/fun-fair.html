Pokeheroes Fun Fair Corn Maze

1. Click on the above button "Hide/Show maze path".
2. Scroll to the bottom of the maze.
3. The square at the bottom middle is the entrance.
4. Every block that looks like the one below is one maze picture in Pokeheroes. The arrows are the directions that you can possibly move through the maze. Click on the directions that you can move to mark it as path.


5. If you want, you can also keep track of what you have already found in the maze today under "What have you found today?" further down the page.

note: reloading the page resets everything

What have you found today?

/1 Cornlet egg

/12 Pokemon fought


  • if you don't fight, you can't progress in the maze
  • if you win, you get 1 Fair Ticket and the Pokemon leaves
  • if you loose, you leave the maze and have to re-enter from the start (no additional entry fee)

/8 Pokeballs containing a singular item

  • x Fair Ticket (4 out of 8 Pokeballs contain a Fair Ticket)

  • x random Gem

  • x random Berry

  • x random Mystery Key

  • x Pokeball

  • x Tiny Mushroom

  • x random Cosplay Box

  • x other

?/5 Stickers

  • you get 4 Fair Tickets every time you find a Sticker
  • if you have found all 5 Stickers and then leave the maze through the exit, you get 25 Fair Tickets
